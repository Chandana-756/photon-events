Radium Gala

About the Event:

Radium Gala is designed to celebrate the fascinating phenomenon of luminescence in nature. This includes a 20-minute period for relaxation and enjoyment. Participants can engage in various stalls, such as a photobooth, grab-and-glow station, memory imprints tattoo palette, dark-tic-tac-toe, cup sacking, ring toss fun and a science experiment display. This event promises to be an engaging and educational experience, offering a unique blend of entertainment and scientific exploration.
